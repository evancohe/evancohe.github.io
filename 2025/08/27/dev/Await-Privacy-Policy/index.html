Await Privacy Policy

Developed by AwaitCR Development Team

Introduction

Await respects and protects the personal privacy rights of all users of our service. To provide you with more accurate and personalized services, this privacy policy explains how Await collects, uses, and protects your information.

Information Collection

Information We Collect

Await primarily collects the following information:

  • Information You Provide: Countdown information, titles, dates, and other data you create while using Await
  • Device Information: Basic device information such as device type and operating system version
  • Usage Data: Anonymous usage data such as app usage patterns and feature usage frequency

How We Collect Information

  • When you create and manage countdowns using Await
  • Automatically through in-app feature usage
  • Through Apple’s App Store and iCloud services

Information Usage

We use the collected information for:

  • Providing and improving Await’s core functionality
  • Personalizing user experience
  • Technical support and problem resolution
  • App functionality optimization and performance improvement
  • Ensuring service security and stability

Information Storage

Storage Locations

  • Local Storage: Your data is primarily stored locally on your device
  • iCloud Sync: If you enable iCloud, data will sync to your iCloud account
  • Security Measures: We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data

Data Retention

  • Your data will be retained until you actively delete it or uninstall the app
  • If you use iCloud, data will be retained according to Apple’s iCloud policy

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties, unless:

  • With your explicit consent
  • Required by law or government agencies
  • To protect our rights and property
  • Necessary sharing related to Apple services (such as iCloud sync, App Store payments)

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Using industry-standard encryption technology
  • Regular security reviews and updates
  • Restricted data access permissions
  • Collaboration with Apple to ensure iCloud data security

Third-Party Services

Await uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple Services: iCloud sync, App Store payments, push notifications
  • Analytics Services: Anonymous data analysis for improving app performance

Await Pro Subscription Service

Subscription Information Collection

When you subscribe to Await Pro, we collect:

  • Subscription Status: Your subscription status and expiration date
  • Payment Information: Processed through Apple, we do not directly store payment details
  • Usage Statistics: Pro feature usage (anonymized)

Subscription Management

  • Subscription Management: Managed through Apple’s subscription system
  • Renewal Reminders: Send reminders before subscription expires
  • Feature Access: Control Pro feature access based on subscription status

Subscription Cancellation

  • You can cancel your subscription through Apple’s subscription management
  • You can continue using Pro features until the current subscription period ends
  • Your data will not be lost after cancellation, but Pro features will be restricted

Children’s Privacy

Await is not specifically designed for children. We do not use any means to store any user’s data. Your data has only two options: local storage and iCloud:

  • Local Storage: All data is stored only on your device
  • iCloud Sync: If you enable iCloud, data will sync to your iCloud account

We do not use any third-party servers or cloud storage services. Regardless of user age, we do not collect, store, or process any personal information. If you are a parent or guardian and find that your child has used Await, your child’s data is stored only on your child’s device or iCloud account. You can control this data through device settings or iCloud management.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and view your data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Manage your subscription settings
  • Export your data

Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy. For significant changes, we will:

  • Notify you within the app
  • Update the “Last Updated” date on this page
  • Inform you through App Store update descriptions

Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about this privacy policy, please contact us:

  • Email: evsdevsubs@outlook.com
  • Contact us through App Store reviews

This privacy policy applies to all versions and platforms of the Await app.

Await 隐私政策

由 AwaitCR开发团队 开发

引言

Await 尊重并保护所有使用服务用户的个人隐私权。为了给您提供更准确、更有个性化的服务，本隐私政策说明了Await如何收集、使用和保护您的信息。

信息收集

我们收集的信息

Await 主要收集以下信息：

  • 您提供的信息：您在使用Await时创建的倒数日信息、标题、日期等数据
  • 设备信息：设备类型、操作系统版本等基本设备信息
  • 使用数据：应用使用情况、功能使用频率等匿名使用数据

信息收集方式

  • 当您使用Await创建和管理倒数日时
  • 通过应用内功能使用情况自动收集
  • 通过Apple的App Store和iCloud服务

信息使用

我们使用收集的信息用于：

  • 提供和改进Await的核心功能
  • 个性化用户体验
  • 技术支持和问题解决
  • 应用功能优化和性能提升
  • 确保服务的安全性和稳定性

信息存储

存储位置

  • 本地存储：您的数据主要存储在您的设备本地
  • iCloud同步：如果您启用iCloud，数据会同步到您的iCloud账户
  • 安全措施：我们采用行业标准的安全措施保护您的数据

数据保留

  • 您的数据会保留到您主动删除或卸载应用
  • 如果您使用iCloud，数据会按照Apple的iCloud政策保留

信息共享

我们不会出售、交易或转让您的个人信息给第三方，除非：

  • 获得您的明确同意
  • 法律要求或政府机构要求
  • 保护我们的权利和财产
  • 与Apple服务相关的必要共享（如iCloud同步、App Store支付）

数据安全

我们采取适当的安全措施保护您的信息：

  • 使用行业标准加密技术
  • 定期安全审查和更新
  • 限制数据访问权限
  • 与Apple合作确保iCloud数据安全

第三方服务

Await 使用以下第三方服务：

  • Apple服务：iCloud同步、App Store支付、推送通知
  • 分析服务：用于改进应用性能的匿名数据分析

Await Pro 订阅服务

订阅信息收集

当您订阅Await Pro时，我们收集：

  • 订阅状态：您的订阅状态和到期时间
  • 支付信息：通过Apple处理，我们不直接存储支付详情
  • 使用统计：Pro功能的使用情况（匿名化）

订阅管理

  • 订阅管理：通过Apple的订阅系统管理
  • 续费提醒：在订阅到期前发送提醒
  • 功能访问：根据订阅状态控制Pro功能访问

订阅取消

  • 您可以通过Apple的订阅管理取消订阅
  • 取消后仍可使用至当前订阅期结束
  • 取消后数据不会丢失，但Pro功能将被限制

儿童隐私

Await 不专门针对儿童设计。我们没有使用任何手段存储任何用户的数据。您的数据只有本地存储和iCloud两种选项：

  • 本地存储：所有数据仅存储在您的设备上
  • iCloud同步：如果您启用iCloud，数据会同步到您的iCloud账户

我们不使用任何第三方服务器或云端存储服务。无论用户年龄如何，我们都不会收集、存储或处理任何个人信息。如果您是家长或监护人，发现孩子使用了Await，孩子的数据仅存储在孩子的设备或iCloud账户中，您可以通过设备设置或iCloud管理来控制这些数据。

您的权利

您有权：

  • 访问和查看您的数据
  • 更正不准确的信息
  • 删除您的账户和数据
  • 管理您的订阅设置
  • 导出您的数据

隐私政策更新

我们可能会更新此隐私政策。重大变更时，我们会：

  • 在应用内通知您
  • 更新此页面的”最后更新时间”
  • 通过App Store更新说明告知您

联系我们

如果您对本隐私政策有任何问题或建议，请联系我们：

  • 邮箱：evsdevsubs@outlook.com
  • 通过App Store评论联系我们

本隐私政策适用于Await应用的所有版本和平台。
