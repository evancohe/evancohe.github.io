Terms of Use

Provided by Kake Journal Development Team
Effective Date: January 21, 2026

1. Service Overview

Kake Journal (hereinafter referred to as “Kake Journal” or “Service”) is a mobile application provided by Kake Journal Development Team, designed to help users write journals, organize entries, and build consistent writing habits.

2. Service Usage

2.1 Service Scope

  • Create and manage journals and entries
  • Track writing progress and history
  • Provide multiple display styles and themes
  • Support desktop widget functionality
  • Provide iCloud data sync (optional)

2.2 Usage Requirements

  • Requires iOS 17.0 or higher
  • Requires a valid Apple ID (for iCloud sync)
  • Requires internet connection (for App Store download and iCloud sync)

3. Data Storage and Privacy

3.1 Data Storage

  • All user data is stored only on your device locally
  • If you enable iCloud, data will sync to your iCloud account
  • We do not use any servers or cloud storage services
  • We do not collect, transmit, or process any user data

3.2 Data Security

  • Data security is protected by your device security settings
  • iCloud data is protected by Apple’s security measures
  • We cannot access your data

3.3 Data Backup

  • You are responsible for regularly backing up your data
  • We recommend enabling iCloud backup to ensure data security
  • We are not responsible for losses due to device loss, damage, or data loss

4. Kake Journal Pro Service

4.1 Service Content

Kake Journal Pro provides the following additional features:

  • Unlimited journal creation
  • Advanced themes and style options
  • Priority customer support
  • Other premium features

4.2 Purchase Options

Kake Journal Pro offers three purchase options:

4.2.1 Monthly Subscription

  • Pay monthly with automatic renewal
  • Can be cancelled at any time, with access continuing until the end of the current subscription period
  • Managed through Apple’s subscription system

4.2.2 Annual Subscription

  • Pay annually with automatic renewal
  • Offers discounted pricing compared to monthly subscription
  • Can be cancelled at any time, with access continuing until the end of the current subscription period
  • Managed through Apple’s subscription system

4.2.3 One-Time Purchase

  • Pay once for permanent access to Kake Journal Pro features
  • No recurring fees, lifetime access with one purchase
  • Applies to all future versions of Kake Journal Pro features
  • Purchased through Apple’s App Store

4.3 Subscription Management

  • Monthly and annual subscriptions are managed through Apple’s subscription system
  • Subscriptions automatically renew unless cancelled before the end of the subscription period
  • You can cancel your subscription through Apple’s subscription management
  • After cancellation, you can continue using Pro features until the current subscription period ends

4.4 Refund Policy

  • Refund requests must be processed through Apple’s refund system
  • We follow Apple’s refund policy
  • One-time purchases follow Apple’s App Store refund policy
  • For technical issues, we will provide technical support

5. Free Version Limitations

5.1 Feature Limitations

  • Free version has limitations on the number of journals that can be created
  • Some advanced themes and styles are only available to Pro users
  • Certain premium features are only available to Pro users

5.2 Upgrade Options

  • You can upgrade to Kake Journal Pro at any time
  • All Pro features are immediately available after upgrade
  • Upgrading does not affect your existing data

6. User Responsibilities

6.1 Compliance Usage

  • You agree to use Kake Journal only for lawful purposes
  • You may not use Kake Journal for any illegal activities
  • You may not attempt to hack or modify the application

6.2 Device Requirements

  • You are responsible for ensuring your device meets minimum system requirements
  • You are responsible for keeping your device security updates current
  • We do not guarantee compatibility on all devices

6.3 Data Management

  • You are responsible for protecting your data
  • You are responsible for regularly backing up important data
  • We are not responsible for data loss due to user operations

7. Disclaimers

7.1 Service Availability

  • We strive to maintain high service availability but do not guarantee 100% uptime
  • Service may be temporarily unavailable due to maintenance, updates, or other reasons
  • We are not responsible for losses due to service interruptions

7.2 Data Accuracy

  • We strive to ensure application accuracy but do not guarantee complete error-free operation
  • You are responsible for verifying the accuracy of important information
  • We are not responsible for losses due to data errors

7.3 Third-Party Services

  • Kake Journal uses Apple’s iCloud service for data synchronization
  • We do not control the availability or security of third-party services
  • We are not responsible for losses due to third-party service issues

8. Intellectual Property

8.1 Our Rights

  • Kake Journal and all its content are protected by intellectual property laws
  • We reserve all related rights
  • You may not copy, distribute, or modify Kake Journal without permission

8.2 Your Rights

  • You may use Kake Journal’s features normally
  • You own all rights to the data you create
  • You may export your data

9. Service Changes and Termination

9.1 Service Changes

  • We may update or modify the service
  • Significant changes will be notified to users in advance
  • Continued use constitutes acceptance of changes

9.2 Service Termination

  • You may terminate the service by uninstalling the application
  • We may terminate the service when necessary
  • After service termination, your data will remain on your device

10. Dispute Resolution

10.1 Applicable Law

  • These terms are governed by the laws of the jurisdiction where Kake Journal Development Team operates
  • Any disputes should be resolved through friendly negotiation

10.2 Contact Information

For disputes or questions, please contact us:

  • Email: evsdevsubs@outlook.com

11.1 Update Notifications

  • We may update these terms
  • Significant updates will be notified to users through the application
  • Continued use constitutes acceptance of updated terms

11.2 Effective Date

  • These terms are effective from January 21, 2026

Kake Journal 使用条款

1. 服务概述

Kake Journal（以下简称”Kake Journal”或”服务”）是由Kake Journal开发团队提供的移动应用程序，旨在帮助用户记录日记、整理条目并建立持续写作习惯。

2. 服务使用

2.1 服务范围

  • 创建和管理日记与条目
  • 追踪写作进度和历史
  • 提供多种显示样式和主题
  • 支持桌面小组件功能
  • 提供iCloud数据同步（可选）

2.2 使用要求

  • 需要iOS 17.0或更高版本
  • 需要有效的Apple ID（用于iCloud同步）
  • 需要互联网连接（用于App Store下载和iCloud同步）

3. 数据存储和隐私

3.1 数据存储

  • 所有用户数据仅存储在您的设备本地
  • 如果您启用iCloud，数据会同步到您的iCloud账户
  • 我们不使用任何服务器或云端存储服务
  • 我们不收集、传输或处理任何用户数据

3.2 数据安全

  • 数据安全由您的设备安全设置保护
  • iCloud数据由Apple的安全措施保护
  • 我们无法访问您的数据

3.3 数据备份

  • 您有责任定期备份您的数据
  • 我们建议您启用iCloud备份以确保数据安全
  • 我们不负责因设备丢失、损坏或数据丢失造成的损失

4. Kake Journal Pro 服务

4.1 服务内容

Kake Journal Pro提供以下额外功能：

  • 无限制的日记创建数量
  • 高级主题和样式选项
  • 优先客户支持
  • 其他高级功能

4.2 购买形式

Kake Journal Pro提供三种购买形式：

4.2.1 月付订阅

  • 按月付费，每月自动续费
  • 可随时取消，取消后仍可使用至当前订阅期结束
  • 通过Apple的订阅系统管理

4.2.2 年付订阅

  • 按年付费，每年自动续费
  • 相比月付享有优惠价格
  • 可随时取消，取消后仍可使用至当前订阅期结束
  • 通过Apple的订阅系统管理

4.2.3 买断制

  • 一次性付费，永久使用Kake Journal Pro功能
  • 无需续费，一次购买终身有效
  • 适用于所有未来版本的Kake Journal Pro功能
  • 通过Apple的App Store购买

4.3 订阅管理

  • 月付和年付订阅通过Apple的订阅系统管理
  • 订阅会自动续费，除非您在订阅期结束前取消
  • 您可以通过Apple的订阅管理取消订阅
  • 取消后仍可使用至当前订阅期结束

4.4 退款政策

  • 退款请求需通过Apple的退款系统处理
  • 我们遵循Apple的退款政策
  • 买断制购买遵循Apple的App Store退款政策

5.2 升级选项

  • 您可以随时升级到Kake Journal Pro
